A complete **Gym Management System** built as an Odoo module.

---

## ✨ Features

### Members Management
- Member profiles with codes, personal info, and photos
- Automatic age calculation
- Membership state tracking (New, Active, Expired, Frozen, Cancelled)
- Financial summary (Total paid, Outstanding, Refunded)

### Subscription Plans
- Flexible plans with duration, price, and freeze days
- Includes: Pool, Group Classes, Personal Training
- Plan codes and descriptions

### Memberships
- Link members to subscription plans
- Automatic end date calculation
- Freeze / Unfreeze logic
- Payment tracking and balance due

### Classes & Bookings
- Group fitness classes with coaches and specializations
- Capacity management & available spots
- Booking system with waitlist
- Class attendance tracking

### Coaches
- Coach profiles with certifications and specializations
- Experience tracking

### Operations
- Check-in / Check-out with duration calculation
- Payment recording (Cash, Card, Bank Transfer, Online)
- Payment wizard for quick transactions
- Automatic membership expiration via cron job
